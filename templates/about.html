About Disease Prediction Model

Our disease prediction model is the result of collaborative efforts by VIT Bhopal students, led by Dr. Sanat Jain. It utilizes advanced machine learning techniques to analyze various health parameters and predict the likelihood of specific diseases.

Through extensive research and data analysis, our model has achieved high accuracy in disease prediction, empowering individuals to take proactive measures for their health.

The model is continuously updated with the latest medical research and data to ensure its effectiveness and relevance. Our goal is to improve healthcare outcomes by providing individuals and healthcare professionals with valuable insights into potential health risks.

We are committed to promoting preventive healthcare and empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their well-being.

Submitted to Dr. Sanat Jain
